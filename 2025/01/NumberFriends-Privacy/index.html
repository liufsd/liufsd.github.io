Number Friends Privacy Policy

Jan 15, 2025

Number Friends is designed for young learners. We keep data collection to an absolute minimum and follow Apple's App Store Review Guidelines, COPPA, and GDPR principles. This document describes what the app collects, how the information is used, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Free play counter: We store an anonymous counter locally on the device to remember how many free sessions have been used. This value is kept in the iOS Keychain so it survives reinstallations. It never leaves the device.
  • Subscription receipts: Apple provides us with encrypted receipts so we can verify active subscriptions. We do not receive payment card data or personal billing details.
  • Crash diagnostics (optional): If you opt in to share analytics with developers via iOS settings, Apple may send anonymized crash reports. Those reports help us fix bugs and do not include personally identifiable information.

How We Use the Data

  • Unlock premium content when a valid subscription is detected.
  • Determine whether a player still has free practice sessions left.
  • Analyze app stability and fix crashes (if diagnostics are shared).

Data Storage & Sharing

All gameplay information stays on the device. We do not operate a separate cloud service for Number Friends, nor do we share data with advertisers or analytics networks. Subscription receipts are verified using Apple's StoreKit APIs, and any verification happens locally within the app.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for children with guidance from their parents or teachers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The parental dashboard and subscription prompts are behind soft gates to prevent accidental taps.

Subscriptions & In-App Purchases

Number Friends offers an auto-renewable subscription to unlock unlimited practice and premium content. Payments are processed through Apple and tied to your Apple ID. You can manage or cancel the subscription anytime in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. If a subscription expires, the app reverts to the free experience.

Your Choices & Rights

  • You can reset the app data by deleting the app and removing it from your backup, though the free-play counter may remain because it is stored securely in the Keychain.
  • You can opt out of diagnostics sharing in Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements.
  • For any data-related questions, contact us at liupeng.skstore@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this document as the app evolves. The latest version will always be available at this URL. When we make significant changes, we will note the update date inside the app.
